Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a bathroom remodel cost in North DFW?

From our recent projects: tub-to-shower conversions typically run $10,000–$14,000, and full bathroom remodels $12,000–$22,000 — tile and fixture choices move the number most. Every quote is a written fixed price, not a ballpark that grows later.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Most bathrooms take about 2 weeks of on-site work. From first visit to finished bathroom — including design choices and material ordering — plan on 5–7 weeks.

Do you use real tile or acrylic liner systems?

Real tile. We build custom tiled showers rather than covering your bathroom with glue-on acrylic panels. Tile costs about the same once you compare written quotes, and it's what keeps its value.

Is the in-home quote really free?

Yes — the visit takes about 30 minutes, and you get a written fixed price that's yours to keep even if you wait or get other bids.
